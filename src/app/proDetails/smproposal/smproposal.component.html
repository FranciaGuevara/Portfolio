Description

Worked with Melange Creperie to help increase their sales by using social media as a method to grab attention from different platforms.

Mission

To increase followers on Instagram and TikTok for Melange by creating content of the foods, drinks and scenery. Houstonians and visitors coming to Houston were the target audience that we wanted to reach.
